### Course Introduction
Working with data is a base necessity in today's world, and databases are the main tool for managing and organizing data. To communicate with databases and manipulate data, we need SQL that is one of the most popular programming languages in the world. This course will teach you everything you need to know about SQL and databases, taught by two industry experts: Mo Binni and Andrei Neagoie.
